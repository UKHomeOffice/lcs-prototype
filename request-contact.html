Request a Notice

If you have reasonable cause to believe that a person is disqualified from renting due to their immigration status you can request a Notice of Letting to a Disqualified Person from the Home Office to confirm this. You can then use this to help you end the tenancy.

To do this, please contact this number:

  • 0300 069 9799
  • Monday to Thursday, 9am to 4:45pm
  • Friday, 9am to 4:30pm
  • Find out about call charges
