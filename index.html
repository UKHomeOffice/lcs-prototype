Request help to evict tenants due to immigration status

Use this service if you suspect the tenants living in the residential property that you own, handle or represent are not legally allowed to stay due to their immigration status.

Warning You can be fined up to Â£3,000 if you knowingly allow illegal tenants to live on your property

This service will allow you to:

  • request a Notice of Disqualification from the Home Office so tenants can be given notice to leave the property
  • check whether a current Notice of Disqualification is still valid
  • report the tenant has left the property

Who can use this service

  • landlords
  • agents acting on behalf of the landlords
  • in some cases, the tenant if he/she is subletting to someone else

There must be written evidence of who will be responsible for issuing disqualification notice.

Before you start

You will need the following:

  • tenant's details including name, date of birth and nationality
  • other tenants/occupants' details residing within the same tenancy who are over the age of 18
  • tenancy start date
  • property address
